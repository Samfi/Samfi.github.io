About Me


I have had a fascination with computers and music since a young age. I began to teach myself to program in 2014. To further my knowledge of programming I now play a key role in a hackEDU club and enjoys learning with other people. I recently worked on a game entry for Ludum Dare and am currently working a replacement clock app for iOS and Android. I Originally learnt the saxophone in 2007 and then moved on to play a wide variety of instruments including my favourite, guitar.

Projects

Second

We are currently building a replacement clock app for phones and tablets alike which will revolutionise the way people see time on their phones.The app is scheduled to be released sometime in October.

Esoteric Adventures

You play as a hippie who has traveled to the Amazon Rain-forest in the search of enlightenment however when you get to the forest you discover it is being cut down by a foresting corporation. You must defeat the corporation and move it out of the forest. The game continues to other levels where you progressively work toward ruling a harmonious world. Built this game in 24 hours for Ludum Dare. I created the audio, made the art and helped largely to program the game.
